How it works

Simple, private, fast

STEP 01
Install once
Add Clearweb to Chrome or Firefox in seconds. No account, no sign-up, no email address needed.
STEP 02
Browse normally
Clearweb runs silently in the background on every page you visit. AI elements fade out before you even see them.
STEP 03
Toggle any time
Click the icon to see how many elements were removed, or pause blocking on any site with one click.
